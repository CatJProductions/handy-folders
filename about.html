Handy Folders | About

Here you will find all sorts of information about Handy Folders.

Pricing

Handy Folders typically prices origami made with normal paper at $0.40, though more fancy paper costs $1.00.

Charity

We do sometimes donate earnings to charity. Our main focus currently is recovery from recent hurricanes.
