Book of December

Space Case, by Stuart Gibbs

December's book of the month is Space Case, by Stuart Gibbs.

A twelve-year-old named Dash Gibson lives on the first colony on the moon, but it isn't as splendid as it may seem. Things get worse when a scientist mysteriously dies, and Dash is suspicious of what happened.
